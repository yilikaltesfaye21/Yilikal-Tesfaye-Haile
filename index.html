About Me

I'm a data analyst and statistical consultant with over 7 years of experience, specializing in experimental design, data analysis, and research methodology. With an M.Sc. in Statistics from Hasselt University, I help researchers and organizations navigate complex data challenges.

My research interests include longitudinal data analysis, survival analysis, and multivariate and hierarchical data analysis. I've collaborated with professionals across numerous fields including health sciences, economics, and social sciences.
